**LLM assessment:** Not generic AI slop — committed liquid-glass identity, disciplined text-safe status tokens, verdict-led copy as a system. ## Overall Impression

The fixes landed: detector clean, role-gating works, verdict-led copy reads as a system, the Guide behaves, empty states teach, and the trust rail (armed bulk keep, evidence-first modal, native dialog) is genuinely good. The remaining gap is concentrated in one place: the leadership *history* path (/variance) is still inventory without interpretation, and two cross-cutting physical issues (sticky nav legibility, campus-color contrast on active controls) erode the boardroom promise. Single biggest opportunity: make the campus — not the date — the hero of every card, and make the delta the loudest number on history pages.

## What's Working

1. **The non-production empty state in Triage** — names the count, explains why nothing is actionable ("a rehearsal or special event"), and offers a one-click jump to the most open work.
2. **Verdict-led copy as a system** — statusLabel/verdictLabel, "All items are clear. 24 good to go.", "Fresh numbers land Sunday evening" — principle 1 implemented in code.
3. **Invisible craft** — text-safe status colors verified (over 5.3:1, under 5.1:1, review 5.3:1, ink-70 5.8:1), tabular-nums, coarse-pointer touch targets, native <dialog>, tuned focus rings, reduced-motion fallback.

## Priority Issues

1. **[P1] Glance card hierarchy inversion.** All four cards' h2 is the identical date; the campus is 11px micro-caps. Pastor Dave scans for his campus and gets four copies of a date he already knows. Fix: campus name as card title; the shared Sunday once in the hero. → layout
2. **[P1] Sticky glass nav fails legibility.** rgba(255,255,255,0.42) + blur lets content ghost through wordmark/tabs on every scroll; nav wraps two rows at <640px with Sign out over content. Fix: scrolled-state opacity ~0.75–0.85, boundary hairline, prevent the wrap. → polish/adapt
3. **[P1] Viewer history is inventory without interpretation.** Date rows carry no over/under signal; detail deltas are muted gray (least emphasized number); baseline silently switches between "working target" (Glance) and "plan" (variance) — same service reads +6:27 there and +9:17 here. Fix: color-coded deltas, verdict chip per date row, explicit baseline labels. → colorize/clarify
4. **[P2] Attention counts steer to dead ends.** Non-production plans are included in per-date counts and "Worst outstanding," which ping-pongs between 2026-04-05 and 2026-05-31. Fix: exclude non-production plans from steering, or render their items as a flat actionable list. → harden
5. **[P2] Contrast on active/status micro-text.** Campus colors as text on white (2.0–3.1:1), --ink-disabled legend text 2.15:1, teal/amber eyebrows 4.0–4.3:1 at 11px tracked caps. Fix: text-safe variants for campus colors and eyebrow tints, same treatment already given to over/under/review. → audit/polish
